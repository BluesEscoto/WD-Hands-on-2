Zed. Zed (birth name Govos and later given the name Usan) also known as The Master of Shadows, is an anti-heroic playable champion in the multiplayer online battle arena game League of Legends. Adopted by the Kinkou Order's leader Master Kusho, Zed trained alongside his master's son Shen.
