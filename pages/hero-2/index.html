Vayne The Night Hunter

  • Information Shauna Vayne is a deadly, remorseless Demacian monster hunter, who has dedicated her life to finding and destroying the demon that murdered her family. Armed with a wrist-mounted crossbow and a heart full of vengeance, she is only truly happy when...
  • Abilities Passive: NIGHT HUNTER Vayne ruthlessly hunts evil-doers, gaining Move Speed when moving toward nearby enemy champions.
  • TUMBLE Q Skill Vayne tumbles, maneuvering to carefully place her next shot. Her next attack deals bonus damage.
  • BOLTS W Skill Vayne tips her bolts with a rare metal, toxic to evil things. The third consecutive attack or ability against the same target deals a percentage of the target's max health as bonus true damage.
  • CONDEMN E Skill Vayne draws a heavy crossbow from her back, and fires a huge bolt at her target, knocking them back and dealing damage. If they collide with terrain, they are impaled, dealing bonus damage and stunning them.
  • FINAL HOUR R Skill Readying herself for an epic confrontation, Vayne gains increased Attack Damage, Invisibility during Tumble, reduced Tumble cooldown, and more bonus Move Speed from Night Hunter
